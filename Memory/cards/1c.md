# Nucleosomes are dynamic

The nucleosome is usually thought of as a single static conformation of histones
with DNA wrapped around them combining 2x H2A/H2B dimers and 2x H3/H4 dimers,
but this is not precise. Nucleosomes take a multitude of forms including
hemisomes (1x H2A/H2B, 1x H3/H4), hexasomes (1x H2A/H2B, 2x H3/H4), and so on.
